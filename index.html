Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is SkillExchange really free?

add

Yes! We operate purely on a time-bank system. When you teach a skill for an hour, you earn an hour to spend learning a skill from anyone else on the platform. No money is exchanged!

Do I need to be an expert to teach?

add

Not at all. Everyone has something to share. Whether you're an advanced professional or simply enthusiatic about a hobby, there's a learner out there looking for exactly your level of experience.

How are sessions conducted?

add

Most sessions are conducted virtually using our built-in video streaming tools. You can also connect via third party platforms like Zoom or Google Meet if you and your learning partner prefer.

What if my partner doesn't show up?

add

If a partner fails to join a scheduled meeting, your time credits are automatically refunded by our system. Repeated no-shows are flagged by our moderation team to keep the community reliable.
